About your stay

Arrival

The Washington University campus is about 20 minutes by car from St Louis Lambert International Airport. St. Louis is a focus city for Southwest Airlines, which has a dedicated terminal (terminal 2). All other airlines are based in Terminal 1, which has an iconic copper roof. Rental cars are available, most of which require a shuttle to a nearby location. When mapping your drive to or from the airport to your hotel, be sure to select the appropriate terminal.

Metrolink

Just a 25 minute ride from your hotel, the St. Louis Metrolink departs Lambert Airport every 20 minutes and offers an efficient alternative to taxis.

If arriving in terminal 1, use this guide to reach the MetroLink.

From terminal 2 (Notice the M on the map).

Board the Red Line train and depart at Delmar Loop:

Accommodations

Hotel

All collaborators will be staying at the Moonrise Hotel on the bustling Delmar Loop. The hotel is just minutes away from the Metrolink and a mile away from Washington University in St. Louis (D4 106).

Local food & attractions

Saint Louis has a vibrant and diverse community which offers a wide variety of food and entertainment. You will be staying on Delmar Loop, one of the most popular neighborhoods for food and drink. Additionally, the nearby Forest Park is a great destination to get a quick view of the local culture.
